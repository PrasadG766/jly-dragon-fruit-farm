About JLY Dragon Fruit Farm

Located at Gongopula village, Bheemgal mandal, Nizamabad district (Telangana), our 3-acre farm is developed as an integrated model focusing on healthy dragon fruit production.

3-Acre Trellis Dragon Fruit Farm

  • Trellis method with ~12 ft spacing between rows for better sunlight and airflow.
  • 20√ó30 ft pond in the middle of the field to support irrigation and micro-climate.
  • Focus on pink-pulp varieties known for colour, sweetness and strong market demand.
  • Minimal chemical usage ‚Äì more focus on farm-made organic inputs and integrated farming.

Why our fruits taste better

  • Balanced nutrition using vermicompost and farmyard manure from our own units.
  • Better pollination support with honey bee boxes placed near the field.
  • Careful picking at the right maturity stage for natural sweetness and shelf life.

Integrated Farming Components

Poultry Shed
Poultry droppings are converted into rich manure, reducing chemical fertilizer needs.
Vermicompost Unit
Farm waste is turned into high-quality compost for dragon fruit plants.
Honey Bee Boxes
Support pollination, improve fruit set, and provide honey as an additional income.
Cattle (2 Nos)
Provide dung + urine for organic preparations and basic farm operations.

This integrated model helps maintain soil health, reduce outside inputs and provide you with naturally grown fruits at a fair price.
